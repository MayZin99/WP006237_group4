Our Story

A Tradition

Born in Fire

Since 1996, K'antica Pizzaria has been bringing a taste of Naples to your table. Inspired by the sun-drenched piazzas of southern Italy, every pizza is hand-stretched, made with our carefully nurtured sourdough culture, authentic DOP ingredients and fresh, seasonal produce, and baked in a wood-fired forno at 485°C. We keep it simple—no shortcuts, no compromises—just tradition, care, and a love for great pizza.
